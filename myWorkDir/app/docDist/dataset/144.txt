{{SbcIntroductionSpanishPod}}
Sometimes people are just too slow! And then they hurry too much! 
In this lesson JP and Liliana teach us how to create time pressure and how to react to it 
and—most importantly—to relax. The keyword is '''tranquila''' – relaxed (for feminine nouns).
