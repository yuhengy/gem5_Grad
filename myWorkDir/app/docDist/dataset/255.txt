= History of Amazon =
On July 16, 1995, Amazon.com delivered its first book through an online transaction.  However, there was far more to Amazon than just a book delivery service.  Its founder, Jeff Bezos, had a vision from the very start: he would revolutionize the use of the internet to do business.<ref name="Amazon Opens for Business">[https://www.history.com/this-day-in-history/amazon-opens-for-business Amazon Opens for Business]</ref>  He registered “Amazon.com” in 1994, named after the south American river.<ref name="A Very Brief History of Amazon: the Everything Store">[https://interestingengineering.com/a-very-brief-history-of-amazon-the-everything-store A Very Brief History of Amazon: the Everything Store]</ref> The massive river not only reflected to his plans for Amazon's growth, but also prevented Bezos from being constrained to any one market through his brand.<ref name="Amazon Opens for Business"/>
